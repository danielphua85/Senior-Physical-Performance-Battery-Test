Registration & Consent

Safety Guidelines

  • Use a stable chair (no wheels) on a non-slip floor.
  • Wear proper footwear and clear obstacles.
  • Have a caregiver/trainer nearby if you are frail or unsteady.
  • Do not proceed if you feel unwell or have uncontrolled symptoms.
  • Stop immediately if you feel dizziness, chest pain, severe breathlessness, sharp pain, or unsafe.

Consent

This is a non-diagnostic fitness screening and education tool. Results are general indicators and not medical advice.

Test Guide

Setup

  • Place phone on a stable surface (tripod/table) at about chest height.
  • Stand about 2–3 metres away so full body is visible.
  • Ensure good lighting and minimal background movement.

Tests

  • Chair Stand Test (30 seconds or 1 minute)
  • Single-Leg Balance (Left then Right)
Tip: If auto-detection struggles, you can enter reps/time manually.
